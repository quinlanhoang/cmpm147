Experiment 3 - Alternate Worlds

Translated from alternate-dungeon-quinlan-hoang

Description

I am trying to integrate an auto tile generator that takes aspects of a tilemap and represents a dungeon and overworld respectively.

Technical

Rooms are randomly generated of varying sizes and will form pathways connecting them. Chests spawn within the rooms as well as the passageways which are formed by taking two midpoints of separated rooms.
